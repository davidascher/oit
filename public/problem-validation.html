mozilla Open Innovation Toolkit

Problem validation exercise

Objective
Learn/validate your hypothesis and assumptions around your problem and target audience.
Outcome
Build products that have problem-solution fit by pivoting or adapting learnings into development of the product.

Time commitment

5 days

People

2 persons

Equipment/Resources

Budget for a targeted Facebook ad.

Detailed Steps

Early problem validation (false door example):

  1. Create a one page that describes your future product; incl. presenting its value proposition and its main benefits.
  2. Include a “sign-up for more information email” CTA.
  3. Ensure this page or request goes out to as many people in your target audience group as possible - using things like Facebook ads or forums.
  4. Collect emails and follow up with a few of them for interviews to understand:
    • what do users who sign up actually think the product does?
    • who do they think it is for?
    • and can the user understand how they would use it in their life?
