Job to be done

The basic idea of jobs to be done is that we hire products or services to do a job for us. That is we use the services with an outcome in mind, however tacit or explicit that outcome may be. And understanding that job, is what is most important for developing the right solution. The Milkshake example explains the basic concept well. For an understanding of the strategic importance of this concept the simultaneously overserved and underserved article from Capital is a good place to start. To get to jobs to be done, you can use a basic interview method, and focus the conversation on the motivating factors of the solution for the users.
