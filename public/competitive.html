Quick Competitive Review

Objective
  • Understand who else is trying to solve the problem you're trying to solve.
  • Understand who else is trying to solve for the job you have identified.
  • Understand how we can be better at serving a need than our competitors
  • How can we show our target customer that our product is better
Outcome
opportunities to deliver something uniquely valuable (differentiation)
