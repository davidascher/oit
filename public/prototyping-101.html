Prototyping 101

There are several general benefits of prototypes:

  • Communicate ideas effectively
  • Give early shape to rough ideas
  • Secure alignment on features, flow, and experience internally
  • Catch mistakes early (including incorrect assumptions and biases)
  • Iterative development that allows you to get to problem/solution fit faster

The type of prototype you want to build is often linked to the fidelity you need. Later, as the ideas get more concrete, we can build higher fidelity prototypes that get us closer to our MVP.
